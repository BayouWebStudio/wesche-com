About

Precision as
a practice.

I've spent over a decade doing one thing — large-scale geometric and sacred geometry. Mandalas, Platonic solids, mathematical patterns. Every line placed with intention, every session a full day of focus.

Based in Houston, TX. Lately I've been taking the same obsession into painting — working on canvas, figuring out what geometry looks like when it's not on skin.
