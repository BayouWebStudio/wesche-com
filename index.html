About

Precision as
a practice.

For over a decade, Raul Wesche has been one of the most sought-after geometric tattoo artists in the world. Working out of Houston, TX, he specializes in large-scale sacred geometry — mandalas, sacred forms, and mathematical patterns rendered with surgical precision.

His work has taken him across the world — Mexico, Belize, Europe — and drawn clients who fly in specifically for pieces that can't be found anywhere else. Now he's extending that same obsession with form into fine art painting.
