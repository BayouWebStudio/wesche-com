The Artist

For twelve years, I have devoted myself to the sacred art of geometric tattooing, transforming the human canvas with precision, intention, and reverence for ancient patterns.

Based in Houston, Texas, I am the founder of Tattoo Temple Houston—a space where art meets spirituality, where each piece carries deeper meaning than its visual form. My work has gained recognition through @weschetattoo, where 297,000 followers witness the intersection of mathematics and beauty.

Now, I stand at the threshold of transformation. Over the next decade, my journey leads from ink to paint, from skin to canvas. This evolution culminates in March 2027, when Tattoo Temple Houston closes its doors for a grand gallery exhibition—marking not an ending, but a metamorphosis.

At $2,500 per day with bookings months in advance, my tattooing represents mastery achieved. Yet the call to fine art grows stronger. This is my story of artistic evolution, of sacred geometry finding new expression, of an artist answering the deepest creative calling.

The Next Chapter

As I transition from tattoo to fine art, sacred geometry finds new expression on canvas. Each painting explores the mathematical poetry that has guided my work for over a decade, now liberated from the constraints of skin to explore infinite possibilities.

This evolution culminates in March 2027 with a grand gallery exhibition—the transformation of Tattoo Temple Houston into a showcase for this new artistic chapter.
