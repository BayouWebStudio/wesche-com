Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Who We Are

This website is operated by Raúl Wesche, a geometric tattoo artist based in Houston, Texas, working out of Tattoo Temple Houston. This privacy policy covers how we handle information collected through this website (wesche.com).

For any questions, reach us at raulwesche@gmail.com.

What We Collect

We keep it simple. The only personal information we collect is what you provide directly when submitting a booking inquiry:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Any additional details you include in your message (project description, placement, etc.)

We do not collect payment information, run login systems, or store cookies beyond what third-party analytics tools use (described below).

How We Use It

Your information is used for one thing: responding to your booking inquiry and scheduling your appointment. That's it.

We do not use your information for marketing campaigns, newsletters (unless you explicitly sign up), or any automated communications. We will not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for their marketing purposes — ever.

Third-Party Services

This website uses a few external services that may collect data independently:

  • Google Analytics — We use Google Analytics to understand general site traffic (page views, visitor locations, device types). This data is aggregated and anonymous. You can opt out via the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.
  • Instagram — Portfolio images and feeds may be embedded from Instagram. Instagram/Meta may set cookies if you interact with embedded content. See Instagram's Privacy Policy.
  • Pinterest — Some portfolio content may link to or embed from Pinterest. See Pinterest's Privacy Policy.

We don't control what these services collect on their end. Their privacy policies govern their practices.

Data Storage & Security

Booking inquiries are received via email and handled manually. We don't maintain a database of client contacts. Once your appointment is complete, your contact information is kept only as long as needed for our records.

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Ask what information we have about you
  • Request that we delete your information
  • Opt out of any communication from us

To exercise any of these rights, email raulwesche@gmail.com and we'll take care of it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the new version will be posted here with an updated date at the top. We don't anticipate major changes — we're a tattoo studio, not a data company.
