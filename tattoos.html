Work

Large-scale sacred geometry, mandalas, and Platonic solids built over full-day sessions with full focus.

Raúl Wesche is a Houston-based geometric tattoo artist with over 12 years of experience specializing in large-scale sacred geometry, mandalas, and mathematically precise blackwork. His signature style draws from Platonic solids, the Flower of Life, Metatron's Cube, and other foundational geometric forms found across ancient cultures and modern mathematics alike.

Every tattoo begins with a full-day session — no rushing, no compromises. Raúl works exclusively with geometric and sacred geometry designs, building intricate compositions that flow naturally with the body's contours. From full sleeves and back pieces to chest panels and leg wraps, each project is a one-of-a-kind collaboration between artist and client.

Based at Tattoo Temple Houston, Raúl has built a reputation for precision dotwork, seamless linework, and compositions that hold up at every scale. His work has been featured across social media, reaching nearly 300K followers on Instagram alone. Whether you're starting your first geometric piece or adding to an existing collection, Raúl brings the same obsessive attention to detail and deep respect for the art form to every session.
